Chenyang Wang 王晨阳

EdTech and HCI Researcher
"Advancing AI and Education for a Better Future!"

I am a Research Scientist (Collaborateur Scientifique) based in Switzerland🇨🇭, pursuing interdisciplinary research in Human-Computer Interaction, Human-Robot Interaction, and Learning Sciences. My current research explores how dialogue between humans and (embodied) AI systems can foster deeper learning and thinking, as well as how AI can open new possibilities for collaborative learning.

Broadly, I am passionate about designing and evaluating educational technologies that are effective, human-centered, and sustainable in real-world learning environments. My work combines human-centered design, system development, user studies in lab and classroom settings, and data-driven approaches to understand how emerging AI systems can better support both learners and educators.

I received my PhD from the Computer Human Interaction for Learning and Instruction Lab (CHILI Lab) at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL), where I was advised by Prof. Pierre Dillenbourg. I am currently affiliated with the HAIS Lab at BFH, working with Prof. Thiemo Wambsganss, and the MOBOTS Lab at EPFL, working with Prof. Francesco Mondada.

Feel free to reach out to me via email for any questions, collaborations or just to say hi!
